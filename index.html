Welcome to Wiseyak

Wiseyak welcomes you!

Wiseyak is a healthcare technology company focused on building Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) applications to offer unique solutions to "Clinical Decision Support" technology such as medication and treatment suggestions, differential diagnosis (DDx - Diagnosis suggestions) and contextually relevant reference information and clinical guidelines at the point of care.

WiseMD

WiseMD is a cloud-based semantic data platform that can import and catalogue data from certified EMR vendors making it inherently capable of supporting AI features for decision support and making the data far more useful and actionable in the process. WiseMD is both a business intelligence and artificial intelligence platform capable of visualizing the bottomline/KPIs based on ingested data and at the same time turning data into actionable intelligence to provide decision support and advanced search/query capability.

WiseMD's key features include: Ontology based medical record store (AiEMR), Auto-generation of order-sets, patient forms at point-of-care, IOT device integration, Differential Diagnosis, Screening and Image Processing, Cloud based medical record store, Patient ownership of their medical records, Biometric patient identification
